My Background / About Me
I am originally from Southern California and moved up to the PNW in 2020 during the pandemic. I started attending Clark College right away and graduated in 2022. Then I transferred to WSUV where I just graduated with my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science in May of 2024.
During my senior year at WSUV I worked part time as a Coding Instructor at Coding With Kids. In this position I taught a few classes and substituted a couple times teaching Elementary school kids basic Coding concepts and getting them interested in making games using Scratch. I also taught an online class of international high schoolers who had little to no experience with coding and taught them a lot of core coding principles such as data types, conditionals, loops, data structures, input/output, functions, event based programming, and more while using Python.
For hobbies I am really into hiking and making all sorts of things with my 3D printer and I'm trying to learn how to do some 3D modeling for printing.
